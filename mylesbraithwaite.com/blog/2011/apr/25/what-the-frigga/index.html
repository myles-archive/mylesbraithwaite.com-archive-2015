What The Frigga

4 years, 10 months ago in Django, Frigga, Contact Management, and Project.

Frigga is basically a pretty wrapper around Django Contacts so you could run it like a CRM or contact management system for people who aren’t comfortable with the Django Administration application.

Update: Frigga is a character in Journey into Mystery (i.e. Thor).
