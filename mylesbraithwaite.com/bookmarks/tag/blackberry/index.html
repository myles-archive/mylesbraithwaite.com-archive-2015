Fairfax strikes $4.7-billion deal to buy BlackBerry  #
Written on 23rd September, 2013 in BlackBerry.

Fairfax Financial Holdings Ltd. is looking to buy BlackBerry Ltd. (also known as Research in Motion) for $9 USD a share, valued at around $4.7 billion. They will be arranging financing from Bank of America and Bank of Montreal in an effort to keep the company in Canada.

RIM, You’re Done Here  #
Written on 22nd June, 2011 in RIM, and BlackBerry.

Research In Motion is done. They’ll be bought in the next year or so, their products will roll into whoever buys them – Microsoft, most probably – and they’ll go the way of Nokia, Danger, and countless other mobile platforms. They’ll exist independently for a while and then be subsumed. It’s over.

RIM plans to integrate the technology with the PlayBook operating system, giving customers access to Android’s more than 130,000 apps, said the people, who asked not to be identified because the effort isn’t public.
