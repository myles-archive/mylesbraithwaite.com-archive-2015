But how many times are you somewhere without your phone?</p> <p>It has a front and rear facing camera so you can do some video conferencing. Which would most likely be proprietary to the BlackBerry. Hopefully Skype will come out with something soon.</p> <p>If you are going to buy it I would suggest getting some type of Cloud Storage option (like Dropbox) so you don't have to mess around with syncing files (PDF, DOC, Excel, etc.).</p> <ul> <li>Smaller than my iPad (could fit in your jacket pocket).</li> <li>Wirelessly connects to your BlackBerry for Email, Calendar, Contacts, and BBM.</li> <li>Download books using Kobo (the Indigo book store). Amazon has a Kindle app for BlackBerry phones so they also should come out with one for the PlayBook.</li> <li>Has access to Documents To Go, so you can edit Excel, Word, and PowerPoint files (mostly like will cost money ~$20).</li> <li>Has a built in PDF reader.</li> <li>It has both a front and rear facing camera. Most apps look more oriented towards the Enterprise user.</li> <li>Google Talk application so you can connect to Pidgin.</li> <li>Evernote - a great note taking and searching application.</li> <li>I couldn't find a remote desktop client (ex when you are in a meeting you could connect to your desktop).</li> <li>It has support to run Android applications but most Android applications don't run while on Android phones so if I had to guess it would be wonky support at best.</li> <li>Has support for an HDMI-out so you might be able to load a PowerPoint presentation on it and then display it on a projector/television (one that has HDMI support).</li> <li>It has an application similar to iTunes where you could download music, movies and podcasts, but I wouldn't buy anything off it.</li> <li>From what I can tell from the screen shots it looks like the calendar, contacts, and email is pretty much the same design as the phone version.</li> </ul>me@mylesbraithwaite.com (Myles Braithwaite)Thu, 07 Apr 2011 12:08:00 -0400http://mylesbraithwaite.com/blog/2011/apr/7/blackberry-playbook/RIMBlackBerry PlayBookTablet
