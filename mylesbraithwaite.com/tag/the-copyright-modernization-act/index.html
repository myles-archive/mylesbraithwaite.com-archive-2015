The Copyright Modernization Act

Netflix CEO says torrent piracy in Canada down 50 per cent  #
Written on 25th September, 2013 in Copyright, Rdio, The Copyright Modernization Act, and Netflix.

I really wish all the Canadian politicians who are trying to pass The Copyright Modernization Act would look at business like Netflix (and even Rdio) and their positive impact of piracy. Government’s shouldn’t try and pass legislation for failing business direction.
