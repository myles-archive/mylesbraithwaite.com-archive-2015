PodCamp and Org-Mode

5Â years ago in PodCamp Toronto 2011, Conferences, Talks, Org-Mode, GTALUG, and Emacs.

I am really getting excited for this weekend’s PodCamp in Toronto. I have been wanting to start a Podcast for the last five or so years so I am hoping this conference will finally give me the kick in the butt I need.

The second thing is next months GTALUG meeting talk with Sacha Chua on Org-Mode, an Emacs plugin that allows text to be organized into discrete sections that are related in a tree structure or hierarchy, basically an Outliner. I have been using Vim for about seven years and OmniOutliner for the past couple of months so I am really looking forward to how people actually work in the Emacs environment.
